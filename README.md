Oury Health Tech Ltd (OHT) Financial & Operational Optimization Project
Project Overview
This capstone project, completed during my final semester at Conestoga College, delivers a structured business analysis solution to help Oury Health Tech Ltd (OHT) overcome financial and operational challenges. The project analyzes current inefficiencies (As-Is Case) and proposes a strategic future state (To-Be Case) backed by financial modeling, workflow engineering, and executive-level documentation.
Key Components & Deliverables
📈 Financial Modeling & Projections (Microsoft Excel)Formulated data-driven projections for production, manufacturing, and labor costs.Quantified financial bottlenecks and modeled cost-reduction strategies for the "To-Be" scenario.
📐 Facility Layout & Floorplans (Microsoft Visio)Engineered detailed facility floorplans to optimize physical workflows.Redesigned space utilization to reduce labor waste and operational bottlenecks.
📄 Business Analysis & Case Studies (Microsoft Word)Documented thorough As-Is and To-Be case studies detailing organizational challenges and proposed solutions.Compiled comprehensive project documentation, requirements gathering, and strategic recommendations.
📊 Executive Presentation (Microsoft PowerPoint)Formatted a professional pitch deck summarizing the financial impact and operational changes.Visualized complex data and floorplan changes for executive stakeholders.
Core Competencies Demonstrated
Business Process Re-engineering (BPR): Mapping workflow transitions from As-Is to To-Be states.
Financial Data Analysis: Labor and production cost modeling.
Technical Writing: Creating boardroom-ready documentation and project reports.
